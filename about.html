About Me

I work as a full-time, freelance illustrator and have been able to travel the world while supporting myself through my art. Over the last two years I’ve spent time traveling through Iceland in a camper van and cozied up in a studio apartment in Karnataka. I’ve bopped all over karnataka and lived more van-life adventures cruising around Maharashtra. I’ve had more oat-milk lattes then I can count and have spent many late nights working from the road, and have plenty of things to share from these experiences. A travel blog seemed like an inevitable next step!

What to Expect From This Travel Blog

I’ll be sharing places I find – of course – as well as travel tips, recommendations, ideas + more! I also want to tap into my artistic side and share advice, ideas, and recommendations for the fellow creative as well! We’re all multi-faceted humans with lots of interests, so why not get into it all! I’m very excited and eager to start writing and sharing on this blog and I hope you’ll join me for the journey!
